The Cybertruck Mystery

The Disappearance

On January 15th, 2024, Tesla reported the mysterious disappearance of 50 Cybertrucks from their Austin Gigafactory. What makes this case particularly intriguing is that all security footage from the time of the incident shows the vehicles in their parking spots, yet physical verification confirmed their absence.

Key Facts:

  • 50 Cybertrucks vanished between 2:00 AM and 4:00 AM CST
  • Security systems showed no signs of breach
  • All vehicles were fully paid units awaiting delivery
  • Estimated value: $5.7 million

The Crypto Connection

Hours after the disappearance, a mysterious smart contract appeared on the Ethereum blockchain containing encrypted coordinates and what appears to be a digital twin of each missing Cybertruck. The contract's code includes an unusual reference to "Project Looking Glass" and requires a specific sequence of NFT-based keys to unlock.

Blockchain Evidence:

Contract Address: 0x742...f9b3
First Transaction: Block #18934211
Initial Signature: "CYBER_EXODUS_2024"

Latest Developments

A group of blockchain forensics experts has discovered a pattern in the contract's metadata that corresponds to specific locations around Austin. Community members are currently organizing a decentralized search effort coordinated through a dedicated DAO.
